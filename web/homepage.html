Welcome to my homepage! My name is Suzanne Brenchley. I would love to show you some pictures and help you see a little bit into my life and what I do everday.




My husband and I met in May of 2016. We went on one date the day we met, and then Preston had to leave back to his job in Mississippi because he was only in town for his sister's wedding. When he went back to work he talked to me on the phone everyday! After being gone just three days, he put in his two weeks and decided to take a risk and come back and date the girl he thought might be his wife. Well it worked out in our favor and we were married on October 22, 2016 in the LDS DC Temple. It was the most amazing experience and I feel blessed everyday to have married such a loving man.

I get to spend my days playing with sidewalk chalk, playing in the pool, hanging at the zoo, and am always up-to-date on the latest animated movies. It's the best job in the world and I will definitely miss it once I graduate and have to get a "real" job.
